# Mnemo Contributor License Agreements

This file contains the two standard, Apache-style Contributor License Agreements
for the **Mnemo** project (the "Project"):

- the **Individual CLA (ICLA)** — for contributing as yourself, and
- the **Corporate CLA (CCLA)** — for contributing on behalf of an employer or
  other legal entity.

These texts are adapted from the Apache Software Foundation ICLA v2.0 and CCLA
v2.0. Signing a CLA **does not change the Project's license** — Mnemo is and
remains licensed under **Apache-2.0** (see [`LICENSE`](LICENSE)) — and **does not
transfer your copyright**. It grants the Project a clear, durable license to use
and redistribute your contribution.

> For everyday contributions, the per-commit **Developer Certificate of Origin**
> sign-off described in [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md) is sufficient. A
> signed CLA is requested only for substantial contributions, at the
> maintainers' discretion.
>
> **Not legal advice.** If you or your employer need a legal review before
> signing, obtain your own counsel.

**How to sign.** Fill in the applicable agreement below, then submit it by
opening a pull request that adds your name to a `contributors/` entry, or by
emailing a completed copy to the maintainers listed on the
[repository](https://github.com/sattyamjjain/mnemo). "You" (or "Your") means the
copyright owner or legal entity authorized by the copyright owner entering into
this Agreement with the Project.
